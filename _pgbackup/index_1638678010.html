Who We Are

Read More About Our Campaign!

We began our quest in July, 2015 to run a half marathon in every state! Physical challenges aside, we also wanted to tie a charity to this endeavor. As we were researching various organizations, in early 2016, our daughter, Micaela, was diagnosed with Type 1 Diabetes.

We decided to join with Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation (JDRF) to raise funds for the millions of people living with and affected by Type 1 Diabetes (T1D). The money we help raise will help JDRF fund critical research to progressively remove the impact of T1D from people's lives until no one has to fear developing the disease.

Please see how your dollars are providing hope to those living with this 24/7 disease.
